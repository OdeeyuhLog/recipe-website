Pork Adobo

Description

Pork Adobo is one of the most popular Filipino dishes known for its savory taste and how easy and cheap it is to cook. This dish is composed of pork, soy sauce, garlic, onions, and etc.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs pork belly
  • 2 tbsps garlic (minced or crushed)
  • 5 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 4 tbspoons vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbspoon peppercorn
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Combine the pork belly, soy sauce and garlic then marinate for 1 hour.
  2. Heat the pot and put-in the marinated pork belly then cook for a few minutes.
  3. Pour the remaining marinade including garlic.
  4. Add water, whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves then bring to a boil.
    Simmer for 40 minutes.
  5. Put in the vinegar and simmer to 12 to 15 minutes
  6. Add salt to taste.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
