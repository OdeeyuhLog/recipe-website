Chicken Inasal

Description

Chicken Inasal is a popular Filipino grilled chicken dish.
It is composed of chicken and savory grill marinade that consists of soy sauce,
garlic, pepper, etc.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken cut into serving pieces
  • 2 tablespoons ginger minced
  • 2 tablespoons garlic minced
  • 3/4 cup lemongrass chopped
  • 1 cup coconut vinegar
  • 1/2 cup lemon or calamansi juice
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup lemon soda softdrink
  • 1/2 tablespoon ground black pepper

Basting sauce:

  • 3 tablespoons annatto oil atsuete oil
  • 1/2 cup margarine softened
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon lemon or calamansi juice

Steps:

  1. In a freezer bag or large bowl, combine chicken, lemongrass, salt, ground pepper,
    black pepper, ginger, garlic, brown sugar, vinegar, lemon-lime soda, and lemon juice.
  2. Stir or shake the mixture until every ingredient is well incorporated.
    Set aside.
  3. In a bowl, combine margarine, annatto oil, salt and lemon juice then stir.
    Stir. Set aside.
  4. Grill the chicken while basting generous amounts of the margarine mixture.
  5. Transfer the grilled chicken to a serving plate.
  6. Serve with sinamak. Enjoy!
